About Me

I’m a cyber geek from Puebla, México. I love make programs.

I’m a graduate of the faculty of “Computer Science” at the University of Puebla BUAP.

My specialty is the development of applications for iPhone and iPad with Swift and Objetive-C, i love that, but also i love make apps for Android and Web Apps.
